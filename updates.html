Yesterday at the Amarillo City Council meeting, we were presented the first donation to the Transformation Park project (a "big check" in many ways). Before we even knew of their generous intentions, the TP board had decided to name the the day center in honor of their philanthropic work in our community. We are confident we will be sharing numerous transformed life stories because of the work that will occur in the Joe & Laura Street Day Center!! Thank you Joe & Laura for your more than generous gift!
