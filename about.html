Transfomation park began with a vision in 2021 to create better housing for Amarillo's homeless population. The model of the homes we are building have been based on similar efforts taking place in Los Angeles with their homeless population. Transformation Park is a non-profit and run solely off of donations and grants and mostly led by volunteers.


In 2021 Texas had 9.4 homeless for every 10,000 population (32nd in the nation).
Amarillo has 30.1 homeless for every 10,000 population.
Amarillo leads the state in homeless per capita.
Homelessness should not exist in the richest country in the world. Find out more here.
