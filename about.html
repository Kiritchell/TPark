Our Situation

Our community currently leads the state in the number of homeless people per capita. Studies show there are 30 homeless people for every 10,000 residents, more than three-times the state average.

Our Values

We value everyone, regardless of their faith or personal situation. We will lean upon the values of Jesus and serve as He encouraged us to while educating our community about the misconceptions of homelessness. We are innovators and will always try new ways to reach and serve our homeless population while creating a culture of collaboration with our partner organizations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to serve our homeless population with the care and compassion we all deserve by bringing the City of Amarillo, local churches, and the commerce community together to help meet their basic needs.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide a welcoming, safe, caring, and compassionate place for the homeless community to gather and have their basic needs met. We believe that by meeting these basic needs, our clients will be better positioned to transition out of homelessness and into long-term permanent housing.
